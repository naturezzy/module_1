The coolest creature

THE MANED WOLF

The maned wolf is awesome because he doesn't give a hoot about our preconceptions. Compared to the foxes.

its body plan seems exotic and extreme. Not only is this the largest canid found in South America, but it's also unusually tall, standing almost 3.2 feet (1 meter) high at the shoulder. The maned wolf bestrides the continent on legs so long and so slender you'd swear the creature is part gazelle (or werewolf!).
